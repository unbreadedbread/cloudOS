Welcome to cloudOS

cloudOS is a simple way to access the internet.


cloudOS had a major update, with many new features like:

  • Mobile support
  • An improved design (Material 3 using BeerCSS)
  • A new domain (unbreadedbread.cc)

    • Many features are still in development or are planned, like:
  • Loading custom apps without an app addition request
  • Customisation
  • New External Apps (and games)
  • New Internal Apps (maybe games)
